# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data using Power BI to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, category performance, profitability, and regional sales patterns.

## Objective

To help business stakeholders understand sales performance and make data-driven decisions.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* Superstore Dataset

## Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards (Sales, Profit, Orders, AOV, Profit Margin)
* Revenue Growth Trend
* Category Performance Analysis
* Top Revenue Generating Products
* Regional Sales Analysis
* Interactive Slicers
* Business Insights & Recommendations

## Key Insights

* Technology is the highest revenue-generating category.
* West region contributes the highest sales and profit.
* Sales show steady growth over time.
* A small number of products generate a significant portion of revenue.
* Furniture has strong sales but lower profit margins.

## Recommendations

* Increase focus on Technology products.
* Expand marketing efforts in high-performing regions.
* Improve margins within Furniture category.
* Prepare inventory ahead of seasonal demand periods.
